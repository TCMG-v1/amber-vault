§ 02

則The Rules

Three states. Three suits. Three actions per turn. Everything in the game runs on threes — that's the whole law.

states

專Latched

Held in hand, committed but not yet expressed. Face-down. Only you see your latched cards.

states

養Dormant

Placed on the table, face-up, in the shared pool. Available to all players via RECEIVE.

states

化Transformed

Sealed into your scored pile. Out of play. Counted at end. The win condition is three distinct hanzi here.

The Turn — pick one

  1. HOLD — draw one card from the deck into your hand. Hand limit: 5 cards.
  2. RECEIVE — take one card from the dormant pool (養) into your hand. The pool refills at end of turn.
  3. TRANSFORM · PLACE — play a card from your hand onto the table. Resolve the card's C-face rule.
  4. TRANSFORM · SEAL — move a card from the table into your 化 pile. Declare aloud: hanzi · gloss · C signature. The seal teaches.

Winning

ConditionHow to trigger
Seal Three (default)First player to seal three different hanzi into their 化 pile wins.
The Chord (advanced)Seal three cards forming a recognized chord pattern (Void · Operator · Way · Worth).
Empty Deck (fallback)If the deck and pool both empty, the player with the largest 化 pile wins.
