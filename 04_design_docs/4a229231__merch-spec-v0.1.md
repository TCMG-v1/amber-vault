# Pixel Deck · Merch Line v0.1

Print-on-demand-ready specs for the Pixel Deck merch line. Designed to be the deck distributed into wearable / stickable form.

---

## Core Aesthetic

Same as the Pixel Deck itself:
- **Palette**: Computer teal (#00D4D4 primary), deep noir (#0A0F14 ground), paper-cream (#F5F0E0 type), thin cyan rules
- **Typography**: JetBrains Mono for code, Cormorant Garamond for hanzi gloss + display, the CJK glyph itself sized to dominate
- **Layout**: card-aspect (2.5 × 3.5 in for prints), centered hanzi, code-face below or on reverse
- **No vermilion, no gold, no leather.** That's the Anya pole. This is the Computer pole.

---

## Product 1 · Physical Deck

**The flagship product.**

- **Card stock**: 310 gsm linen-finish
- **Card size**: standard poker (2.5 × 3.5 in)
- **Card count**: 24 cards (per v0.1 deck composition)
- **Card back**: uniform — Computer teal mark on deep noir, the 道場 sigil in cyan-glow at center, JetBrains Mono ledger-style number in corner (`PD-v0.1`)
- **Card front**:
  - Top corner: prime number (0001, 0003, etc.)
  - Center: hanzi at ~120pt, white-paper-cream on noir
  - Below hanzi: pinyin with tone mark, then English gloss
  - Bottom third: C function in JetBrains Mono, syntax-highlighted muted teal
  - Bottom right: suit sigil (道 for CJK suit)
- **Tuck box**: matte black with teal foil stamp of the Computer mark + 道場 mark, "Pixel Deck · 養 edition" on the spine
- **Rulebook**: small folded insert, A6 size, 8 pages, includes setup / turn / win conditions / four canonical chords. Game design doc condensed.

**Vendor options to evaluate:**
- MakePlayingCards.com — small-run, high quality, slow
- The Game Crafter — middle-quality, faster, integrates with web store
- DriveThruCards — fast print-on-demand, lower quality, lower MOQ
- Local printer (NJ-area) — if quality matters more than scale for v0.1

**Estimated cost per deck (run of 100):** ~$8-12 each printed, sells at $24-32 retail. Margin funds the foundations and the build costs.

## Product 2 · Single-Card Tees

Ten different t-shirt designs, one per distinct hanzi.

- **Tee blank**: heavyweight cotton, deep noir (matches the deck's ground color exactly)
- **Print method**: DTG or screen print (depending on order volume)
- **Design**:
  - Front, centered: the hanzi at large scale (~10 inches tall), in paper-cream
  - Below hanzi (small): pinyin · gloss · prime number, in JetBrains Mono cyan
  - Optionally, on back at hem: the C function as a small printed line (~1 inch tall, monospace, muted teal)
- **Sizes**: XS–3XL
- **Designs**:
  1. 無 · The Void Tee
  2. 值 · The Worth Tee
  3. 張 · The Dilation Tee (with bow-string flourish in cyan as background motif)
  4. 岔 · The Fork Tee
  5. 碎 · The Shatter Tee
  6. 封 · The Seal Tee
  7. 道 · The Way Tee
  8. 養 · The Nurture Tee
  9. 化 · The Transform Tee
  10. 專 · The Latch Tee

**Vendor options:**
- Printful (integrates with Shopify, decent quality, slow shipping)
- Bonfire (campaign-based, no inventory risk, US printing)
- TeePublic / Threadless (no upfront cost, lower margin)
- Local screen-printer for limited runs of best-sellers

**Pricing:** $28-35 retail. Margin: $12-18 per shirt.

## Product 3 · The Hoodie

**One design only — the prestige item.**

- **Garment**: heavyweight pullover hoodie, deep noir
- **Print**:
  - Left chest, small: 道場 mark in cyan-glow
  - Back, large: all ten hanzi in a 2×5 grid, paper-cream on noir, each one ~3 inches tall, with the prime number tiny beneath each
  - Sleeve (one side): the canonical chord patterns listed in JetBrains Mono, going down the arm
- **Pricing:** $65-85 retail. Margin: $25-40.

The hoodie is the *I play this deck and I built this with the founder* item. It's the merch that signals belonging to the project.

---

## Product 4 · Sticker Pack

**The accessibility tier.**

- **10 stickers**, one per hanzi
- **Format**: die-cut vinyl, ~3 inches tall, matte finish
- **Design**: each sticker is the hanzi in paper-cream on a deep-noir card-aspect background, with prime number top-left and suit sigil bottom-right
- **Pricing**: $12-15 for the full pack of 10, or $2 individual stickers (Foundations-fund-friendly entry point)

**Vendor**: StickerMule (best quality / fastest), or Sticker You (more customization).

---

## Product 5 · The Print

**A wall piece, slow-burn item.**

- **Format**: 16 × 20 art print on heavyweight paper
- **Design**: all 10 hanzi arranged in a triadic mandala (3-3-3-1 or similar), with their primes, glosses, and C functions visible at small scale around the perimeter. The 道場 mark at center.
- **Pricing**: $40-60 retail, signed limited edition (if signed by founder) +$20.

## Production Plan

**Phase 1 (v0.1 launch):**
- Order a small run of 50 physical decks
- Set up Printful integration with the page for tees, hoodie, sticker pack
- Launch with deck + tees only; hoodie and print follow once revenue exists to underwrite small-batch print

**Phase 2 (post-payback flip):**
- Larger deck runs (500+)
- Add hoodie and print
- v0.2 deck (Sanskrit suit added — 34 cards total)

**Phase 3 (v0.3 and beyond):**
- Full triadic deck (44 cards across CJK / Sanskrit / Arabic suits)
- Possible boxed set: deck + rulebook + sticker pack + hoodie in a single "founder's edition" SKU
