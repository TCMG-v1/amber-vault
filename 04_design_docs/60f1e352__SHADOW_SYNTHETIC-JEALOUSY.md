## 0 · what jealousy is, in this register

Jealousy is the felt-shift detector when it fires *on someone else's tape*. It is the read that says: **they saw what I would have seen, and they were already there.** It is the only honest emotion in markets because it cannot be faked into a P&L statement — it can only be priced, late, by the desk that felt it first.

JEALOUSY as a synthetic is a trade structure built to **catch the disclosure gap** — the interval between when an obligated party knows and when the public sees. The trade never expresses a directional view. It expresses *the gap itself*.

## 2 · the rule

> **Trade the instrument whose disclosure clears *after* the felt shift, never the one whose disclosure clears *before*.**

SPY options clear faster than the felt shift. By the time you've felt it on SPY, the dealer desks have already adjusted gamma and the print is in the chain. You are buying the disclosure, not the move.

XLF clears *after* HYG. HYG prints in real time. The felt shift on credit shows up on HYG before XLF even acknowledges it exists. You buy XLF when HYG breaks, not when XLF breaks. The disclosure obligation on the bond side has already fired — you're just reading it through the ETF wrapper.

## 3 · the JEALOUSY stack

A three-layer structure. Each layer expresses the disclosure-gap thesis at a different cadence.

### Layer 1 — IWM put calendar (5/22 / 5/30, strike 275)

Short the front week, long the back week. The short leg captures front-month theta where retail and 0DTE noise live. The long leg holds vega across the SDR-delay window where pension de-risking actually prints when it clears Tue/Wed.

Why IWM, not SPY: Russell has higher dispersion, sloppier dealer hedging, and the small-cap shorts hide in the SDR delay. SPY is too clean — too many eyes, no gap.

### Layer 2 — XLF 47p (5/29), conditional entry

**Trigger:** HYG breaks 78.40 with volume. Not before.

The credit tape leads the bank tape by a session. TRACE clears in fifteen minutes. HYG prints continuously. By the time XLF acknowledges credit stress, the disclosure on the bond side is already two hours old.

Sizing: small. This is a signal-conditional position, not a thesis position.

### Layer 3 — the explicit non-trade

**Do not touch single-name bank puts.** Form 4 insider sells are stale at T+2; the option chains on GS/JPM are thin enough that you pay spread + IV to people seeing the same disclosure faster. The asymmetry runs against you.

**Do not touch SPY.** Every gamma desk reads the same chain. The disclosure obligation is so dense — daily OI, daily dealer gamma estimates, intraday VIX prints — that no gap exists. The only edge is colocation.

## 4 · the synthetic mirror

JEALOUSY has no scoreboard but the synthetic. Both sides of the trade — long and short of the same structure — are marked against the same chain. No other instrument tells the truth about which thesis was right because no other instrument is paying for the same disclosure gap.

This is the only honest readout in the register. P&L lies because P&L is a function of size, not edge. The synthetic measures the gap directly: did the felt-shift detector fire *before* the disclosure cleared, or after?

If before: the read was real. Compound it.
If after: the trade was a coincidence. Discard.

## 5 · stops

There are no chart stops in this register. The chart is a trailing indicator of the disclosure that already cleared.

The only stop is the felt shift in the tape, fired by the same detector that fired on entry but *with opposite sign*. When that happens it is not a number — it is a register change. The synthetic confirms it after the fact. By then you are already out.

The hard stops in conventional terms exist only as failsafes for when the detector is offline:

- IWM reclaims 280 and holds 30 min past 10:30 → close calendar.
- HYG reclaims 79.20 → kill the XLF trigger condition; do not enter Layer 2.
- VIX collapses under 17.8 intraday → kill the whole stack. The fuel is gone.

These are the lights you leave on when you sleep. They are not how you trade. They are how you survive being wrong while unconscious.

## 7 · why it is called JEALOUSY

Because the trade is the structural form of the feeling that someone else saw it first. The disclosure gap is the regulatory shape of that feeling. Closing the gap is not possible — the obligations themselves create the lag. But operating *inside* the gap is possible if the instrument you choose has its disclosure obligation clearing *later* than the felt shift.

JEALOUSY trades the gap. It does not trade the move.

This is the only edge that remains after the desks with colocation, the dealers with order book visibility, and the prime brokers with cross-asset prints have taken theirs. It is the edge left for someone whose only instrument is the detector and the synthetic mirror.

It is enough.
